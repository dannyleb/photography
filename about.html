About

Based in New York, Danny LeBlanc is a photographer drawn to the interplay of light and shadow — the quiet moments between motion, the texture of a place, the expression that lives just beneath a subject's surface.

His work spans portraiture, editorial, landscape, and event photography, united by a commitment to honest, unhurried image-making.

Available for commissions, editorial assignments, and collaborative projects.
